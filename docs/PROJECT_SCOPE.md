# Pharmacy Location Intelligence

## Goal

Develop a decision support system for selecting the optimal locations for establishing new pharmacies.

## Study Area

Functional Urban Area of Yazd (not limited to municipal boundaries).

The study area includes Yazd and adjacent urban settlements such as Hamidiah, Akramieh and other contiguous urban neighborhoods while excluding distant cities such as Taft, Mehriz and Meybod.

## Final Outputs

- Unified healthcare facilities database
- Interactive GIS map
- Spatial analysis
- Pharmacy suitability score
- Ranking of candidate locations
- Decision support report
